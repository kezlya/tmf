# TMF Execution Plan

## The Goal

**Minimum target: EARN $20,000 from TMF.**
Everything above $20K is bonus. This is the "low bar, enjoy life" approach — no pressure beyond the minimum. Achieve this, then enjoy life. Anything on top is gravy.

## The Constraint

- 2+ hours/day dedicated to TMF
- The OpenClaw hype window is open NOW but will shrink
- Speed to market > perfection

## The Pattern to Break

Previous behavior: FOMO → panic → freeze → miss the window.
This time: Plan → execute daily → ship fast → iterate.

The execution plan is designed to produce revenue as fast as possible. No perfection. No "one more feature." Ship, sell, improve.

**Goal: Build the content flywheel. Every day produces content.**

- Post on LinkedIn 4-5x/week

- Each VIP session = 2-3 content pieces

### Content Types
- "How I set up my AI assistant to manage 4 businesses" (origin story)
- "The #1 mistake non-technical people make with OpenClaw" (pain point)
- "Fire Mode vs. Compass Mode: why your AI needs both" (product education)
- Client success stories (with permission)
- Quick tips and screenshots

### VIP Sessions as Content Fuel
- Every $400 session generates:
  - 1 LinkedIn story ("I just helped [persona] set up their AI assistant...")
  - 1 educational post (a specific problem solved during the session)
  - 1 tip/trick post (a quick win from the session)
